University Life And Achivements

Entering to a reputed university was my first priority after finishing my Advanced Level examination. Even though I did not had a crystal clear ambition of becoming a Software engineer, in my opinion, entering to University of Westminster via IIT was the best leap that I have taken in my life so far. Here in IIT I am currently studying software engineering degree as a full time student. I am studying four modules, software development Principle, communication and learning skills, Information and data Modeling and computer system fundamental. At the beginning the flow of this institution was a bit different from what I expected in a so called "UNIVERSITY".

I did ICT for advanced level , but programming languages such as java is giving me a new experience and I would like to define it as a challenging experience .The first coursework was a total nightmare for me, The sleepless nights ,the feeling of loneliness for being away from my family for several weekends wasn’t worthless as I managed to submit it before the deadline .As a person from down south and being a Sinhala medium student was't easy for me when it comes to presentations .I have a little stage fear and I must improve my Communication skills ASAP. Communication and learning skills module is helping and motivate me to recover that. I’m willing to join the rugby team and also the rotaract club. My aim is to perform better in the near future.
